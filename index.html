FAQ

Frequesntly Asked Questions

  • Is there anything my child needs to know before attending?

    This course is made for kids without any knowledge to advanced students. The class is delivered in small student clusters to increase student attention. Each student will go home with something to show at the end of each week.

  • Do I need to bring anything?

    All you need to bring to your session is a laptop and creative imagination. We provide a safe and fun learning environment.
