01. Why code?

STRENGTHEN - Learning to code reinforces your child's logical thinking and problem-solving skills - vital in many different areas of life, from Science and Engineering to Medicine and Law.

INTEGRATE - Learning to code is one of the first sciences that combines art, logic, math, and storytelling.

ENHANCE - Learning to code will increase your child's creative thinking, systematic reasoning, and their ability to collaborate with others
